Introduction

Welcome to the Dark Souls III lore website, home of the storytelling and history of the events in Dark Souls III. Within this site you'll find a variety of information solely focusing on the characters, bosses, and events that occur in Dark Souls III. This site is not a wiki - specific information about the game's mechanics and gameplay elements will not be listed here.

To start, look towards the header ( top of the page ), here you can browse numerous categories depending on what you want to research. To note, this sight and the contents within it should not be interpreted as the developer's intentions for the story/lore of Dark Souls III. This is my interpretation based off of my research - by holding a magnifying glass to in-game architecture and scenery, to reading descriptions of items. Additionally, I advise (if you have not already done so), to play through the game yourself - Dark Souls III (as well as the other games in the series) are rich with content, storytelling, and engaging gameplay.

Finally, as with most media that is inconspicuous and vague, interpretations can vary - I cannot claim any comments here to be one-hundred percent accurate, nor would I want this to be the case. The best interpretation of Dark Soul's lore is that of which the player concludes on their own. Enjoy!
